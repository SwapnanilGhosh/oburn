About oBURN

Introducing Ordinal Burn (oBURN) - the ERC 20 token that's set to revolutionize the world of cryptocurrency. As a burn token, oBURN offers unparalleled security and efficiency to your transactions. With each transaction, a small percentage of the token is burned, reducing the total supply and increasing the value of each remaining token. Say goodbye to inflation and hello to a more stable and valuable investment with oBURN. Join the burn revolution today and experience the benefits of this groundbreaking technology for yourself.
